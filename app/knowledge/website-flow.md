# Website Navigation Guide

## Home Page

The landing page contains a company overview, services, platform expertise, research and insights, locations, and AI assistants.

## Hero Assistant

Located in the hero section of the home page. Its purpose is to answer questions about Open Queue, explain services, guide users through the website, and help visitors find the correct page.

Example questions users can ask:
- What services do you offer?
- Where can I apply?
- How do I request a consultation?

## OpenQ AI Sales Assistant

Found in the section titled "See OpenQ AI in Action." This assistant helps visitors identify which service is best for their needs. It understands customer requirements, asks clarifying questions, recommends suitable services, and provides guidance.

## Research & Insights

Contains research papers, technology insights, AI reports, and innovation articles. Users can expand reports to read full content.
