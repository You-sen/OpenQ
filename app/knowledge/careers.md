# Careers

## Applying For A Job

The Careers page lists all available job openings. To apply, the candidate opens the Careers page, selects a job, views the job details, and clicks Apply to enter the AI Interview Assistant.

From there the candidate completes their profile information, verifies their email via OTP, completes an optional technical screening, uploads their resume, and submits their application.

## AI Interview Assistant

The AI Interview Assistant collects candidate information, verifies email, conducts technical interviews, evaluates responses, collects resumes, and submits applications. Interview scores are never shown to candidates. Technical screening is optional but may help prioritize applications.

## Experience Levels

- 0–1 Years: Fresher
- 1–3 Years: Junior
- 3–7 Years: Mid Level
- 8–11 Years: Senior Level
- 12+ Years: Lead Level
