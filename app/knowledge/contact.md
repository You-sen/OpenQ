# Contact Open Queue

## Contact Page

The Contact page allows visitors to request services, proposals, or consultations. The form collects first name, last name, work email, company name, service interest, and a description of how Open Queue can help.

## Consultation

Users can schedule a free consultation. All submitted requests are stored internally so the Open Queue team can review and follow up with the requester.

## When To Use The Contact Page

Visitors should use the Contact page when requesting a proposal, requesting a quote, scheduling a consultation, discussing project requirements, or exploring partnership opportunities.
